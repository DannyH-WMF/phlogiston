Tranches

This tranche is not expected to ever be completed, but a growth well above the burnup rate could signal a problem. It includes: urgent interruptions, work that should be done before work in any other tranche is done; urgent non-essential work, that should be done soon but does not specifically block or pre-empt planned work; and work to categorize and manage work, because that may lead to interruptive work of the other two types.

This is the current goal for VE work. For completion within the forecast, the backlog should be stable with the burnup steadily increasing toward it.

Significant burnup on Tranches 2 or later may indicate that work should be re-prioritized, since Tranche 1 is considered more important to complete.
